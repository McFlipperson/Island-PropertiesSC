# Island Properties - Luxury Real Estate Platform

## Vision & Target Market
- **Target:** High-net-worth foreign buyers investing in Bohol, Philippines
- **Differentiator:** "Safe Ownership Structures" (Corporations, Leaseholds) to navigate Filipino real estate laws
- **Positioning:** "Concierge Real Estate Platform" - not just selling houses, selling legal structures
- **Aesthetic:** "Old World Wealth meets Tropical Paradise" (White Lotus vibes)
- **Colors:** Deep Emerald Green, Gold, Cream
- **Philosophy:** "Lean Luxury" - high-end aesthetics, mobile-first, zero bloat

## Tech Stack
- **Framework:** Next.js 14 (App Router) + TypeScript
- **CMS:** Sanity.io (headless) - handles data, images, video hosting
- **Styling:** Tailwind CSS + Framer Motion (glassmorphism, smooth transitions)
- **State:** Zustand (currency toggles & modals)
- **Email:** Resend (contact forms & lead magnets)

## Key Features
- **Cinematic Listing Pages:** Mobile-first, sticky video player, swipeable media deck
- **Privacy-First:** NO maps (Google/Mapbox), only text proximity ("✈️ Airport: 12 mins")
- **Currency Logic:** Global PHP/USD toggle
- **Detailed Schema:** Generator, Starlink, Title Type (TCT/CCT), HOA fees
- **Velvet Rope:** Legal details gated behind lead magnets, not public
